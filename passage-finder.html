About

This is a training program to help you get more proficient in handling your Bible. You get a random Bible passage and have to find it in your Bible. If you don't find it, you can increase the passage length.

I have created the program for myself as the most "brutal" no nonsense way for me to help me get more proficient looking up passages in my Bible.

I believe the results are best if you don't give up too easily on a passage and look up every passage in a physical Bible.
